# SOCMINT Investigation & Sanitized Case Study

## **Author:** uriel0byte

## Overview
This repository contains a professional Open Source Intelligence (OSINT) and Social Media Intelligence (SOCMINT) reporting template, alongside a fictional case study built on a fabricated persona.

The purpose of this project is to demonstrate the intelligence-gathering lifecycle, from raw data collection to actionable, structured analysis, while strictly adhering to data privacy and operational security (OPSEC) best practices.

## Scenario & Objective
The included case study (`CONFIDENTIAL - Target Alpha.md`) details a public exposure assessment of a target individual. 
* **Primary Objective:** To map the digital footprint, identify cross-platform username correlations, and assess potential OPSEC vulnerabilities based strictly on publicly available information.
* **Scope:** Passive reconnaissance only. No active engagement, bypassing of privacy controls, or credential harvesting was utilized.

## Scope, Ethics & Legal Boundaries

All investigations documented in this repository follow a 
strict passive-only methodology. The rules below apply 
regardless of whether the subject is fictional or real.

* No accounts were created to access private or restricted content.
* No data breach material, credential databases, or illegal 
  access methods were used at any point.
* Financial OSINT lookups used only publicly accessible 
  verification endpoints.
* All real-world investigations conducted under this methodology 
  require documented consent from the subject or formal legal 
  authority before any collection begins.
* Failed vectors and negative intelligence are documented to 
  establish honest boundaries — not to imply further 
  investigation was attempted.
* The purpose of this work is to demonstrate structured 
  analytical methodology, not to expose or harm any individual.
